02The Logo

One mark, made to stand out.

The Hantrix wordmark leads on every brand surface. Give it room, keep it level, and let the orange do the talking. It reverses cleanly to white on orange, ink, or photography.

Primary — on white
On light grey #F5F5F5
Reversed — on orange
Reversed — on black

Clear space & scale

Keep breathing room around the logo equal to at least the height of the “H” on every side. Never crowd it with text or graphics.

Minimum size — 150 px wide on screen, 28 mm in print. Below that, legibility breaks.

03Logo Integrity

Protect the mark.

✓ Always

  • Use the logo on white, light grey, orange, or black.
  • Scale it uniformly — keep the original proportions.
  • Give it generous, uncluttered clear space.
  • Reverse to white on busy or dark backgrounds.

✕ Never

  • Recolour the logo outside the brand palette.
  • Stretch, skew, rotate, or add drop shadows.
  • Place it on low-contrast or clashing backgrounds.
  • Rebuild the wordmark in a different typeface.

08Application

The payment result screens.

After checkout, students land on one of two states — built entirely from the system above. Both are white cards with a 40px radius, a soft-tinted illustration badge, and the standard pill buttons, shown as a modal over the blurred home page.

Thank You for Your Purchase!

Your order has been successfully placed. Your course is now ready whenever you are.

Oops! Something Went Wrong.

Your payment could not be completed. Please check your details and try again, or contact support.
